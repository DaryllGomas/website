About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Daryll. I hacked this site together to more deeply show the skillsets I have and what I am learning and doing in the tech space as well as life. You probably came here from my resume, but often a resume doesn't quite tell the story. So if thats who you are, welcome.

For me Tech and Learning is a passion. Its in my blood. My Dad was a engineer at IBM and this was a blessing at a young age. I had all the fun gadgets from i386 " super computers " to blazing fast 14k modems. My toys were the cutting edge technologies that he would work with and it gave me a very early head start into the the Tech world that is now unfolding before us. It instilled a forwarding looking perspective that has given me a long career, that im still very passionate about.
